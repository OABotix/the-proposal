Event Details

Faith Nights

Our monthly Faith Nights are gatherings for worship, teaching, and community building. Each month we explore different themes relevant to young Christians navigating modern life.

All are welcome, regardless of where you are on your faith journey.

Open Mic Sessions

A space for creative expression! Share your poetry, music, stories, or any talent you'd like to showcase in a supportive environment.

Sign-ups are available at the door, or you can email ahead to reserve a spot.

Tournaments

We host regular sports tournaments including soccer, basketball, and more. These events promote teamwork, healthy competition, and sportsmanship.

Both individual and team registrations are available.
