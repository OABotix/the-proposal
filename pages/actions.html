Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a difference in young lives.

Mentorship Programs

Become a mentor and provide guidance, support, and friendship to a young person.

Time commitment: 2-4 hours per week

Requirements: Background check, training session, minimum 6-month commitment

Program Assistants

Help facilitate our various programs including creative workshops, sports activities, and tutoring sessions.

Time commitment: Flexible, based on program schedule

Requirements: Skills or experience in relevant area, passion for youth development

Event Support

Assist with organizing and running our community events, from setup to cleanup.

Time commitment: Occasional evenings and weekends

Requirements: Reliability, teamwork skills
